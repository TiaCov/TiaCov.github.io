Projects

Project 1 - Digital Media

This project explored three types of digital media: images, audio and video. Some of the learning objectives included examining the ways that digital media can be used on the web and demonstrating the ability to apply digital media techniques. Though the use of editing software, I was able to create a project around starting a t-shirt line that encompassed all three digital media types and served to provide effective instruction for my peers. A recap of my learning includes: the ability to better use image and text placement in images, the ability to edit audio to enhance the user experience, and the ability to utilize free and paid video programs to create professional looking resources that complement learning.

Reflecting on this project, I feel that my strengths were editing images and audio. I feel that my biggest area of improvement was video. Although I was able to meet the requirements of the project, I feel that there were advanced techniques that I would have liked to use to refine the project. However, time did not permit. Since the completion of the course, I have continued to strive to learn some advanced video editing techniques and skills.

Resources Used:

  • Digital Media Primer (Yue-Ling, 2015)
  • The Non-Designer’s Design Book (Williams, 2015)
  • Adobe Creative Cloud
  • YouTube music library

Project 2 - Research Proposal

The Front-End Analysis and Systems Evaluation competency is meant to provide students the ability to develop skillsets related to data collection, analysis, and evaluation as they apply to designing, developing and learning opportunities and systems.

The project displayed for this competency is a full research proposal. The topic that I chose for this project attempted to answer the question of whether or not interactive learning enhances knowledge absorption and lends to desired behavioral change. This project included background research, a literature review, and a methodology section.This project meets competency 2.1 which states, “Objective 2.1: Students are able to conduct analysis in order to translate and use theoretical frameworks and existing research to design/develop learning opportunities and systems.” This competency was met as I was able to use and explain qualitative and quantitative research methods to demonstrate how they can be used to answer social science research problems. Using these methods, I was able to answer the instructor provided questions and meet the specified project requirements. Additionally, I was able to analyze and address potential ethical issues that can arise in all stages of the research process. Reflecting on the course of completing this project, I realize that I was strong in the categories included in the methodology section. However, if I were to re-do the project, I would refine my research question a bit more as there was not a surplus of existing literature on the chosen topic. I feel that with a more extensive database of literature, I would have been able to better conclude exactly “how”interactive learning impacts long-time knowledge absorption and on the job behavioral change

A recap of my learning includes how to effectively choose qualitative or quantitative methods depending upon what problem you are trying to solve for, how to anticipate ethical concerns in research and, how to formulate a comprehensive research proposal.

To complete this project, I relied on:

  • Research Design: Qualitative, Quantitative, & Mixed Methods Approaches by Cresswell, JW & Creswell, J.D.
  • Ellis Library

Project 3 -

The Leadership and Social Responsibility competency aims to provide students with the ability to become socially responsible and reflective on how technology interacts with society.

The project that I have included that aligns with this competency is the ISD project I helped to create alongside of my group. Our project was called Happiness and Wellness: A Self-Care Course. This project required that each team member manage certain aspects of the project, including research, the development of materials, ethical considerations, any considerations and final findings. My main role was to manage the Learner Analysis, the Contextual Analysis, and the Summative Assessment. I also served an overall editor for the project content. Through this project, we determined our ideal learners, assessed their current knowledge, determined course objectives and designed and developed materials which included assessments, surveys, PowerPoint presentations, other instructor led materials, post-learning resources and reflections

I was able to meet this competency by demonstrating technology leadership and knowledge of the related ethics that had to be considered. I believe my strengths in this project were my ability to take a creative lens to designing surveys, the learner assessment, and ethical considerations. My area of opportunity is the formative evaluation. Through this project, I feel that I really strengthened my ability to effectively solve group conflicts and better assess a learner’s current knowledge.

Resources Used:

  • Cognitive Distortion: When Your Brain Lies To You
  • Exploring the Impacts of Cognitive Distortion
