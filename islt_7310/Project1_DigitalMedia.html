Project 1

Digital Media

This project explored three types of digital media: images, audio and video. Some of the learning objectives included examining the ways that digital media can be used on the web and demonstrating the ability to apply digital media techniques. Though the use of editing software, I was able to create a project around starting a t-shirt line that encompassed all three digital media types and served to provide effective instruction for my peers. A recap of my learning includes: the ability to better use image and text placement in images, the ability to edit audio to enhance the user experience, and the ability to utilize free and paid video programs to create professional looking resources that complement learning.

Reflecting on this project, I feel that my strengths were editing images and audio. I feel that my biggest area of improvement was video. Although I was able to meet the requirements of the project, I feel that there were advanced techniques that I would have liked to use to refine the project. However, time did not permit. Since the completion of the course, I have continued to strive to learn some advanced video editing techniques and skills.
